Who We Are

Built Different

Greatwolf Games is an indie studio built on one belief — great games should be for everyone. We make accessible, ethical games that respect players.

♿
Accessibility First

Full screen reader support built in from day one — not bolted on as an afterthought. Every button, every action, every piece of content is designed to work for everyone.

🚫
No Dark Patterns

No energy timers. No pay-to-win. No gacha. No artificial friction. We believe friction in a game should serve the player experience — never exploit it.

🆓
Free to Play, Always

Every game we make is free. If you want to support us down the line there will be optional ways to do that — but you will never need to pay to enjoy the full experience.

🌍
Play Anywhere

All our games run in your browser. iPhone, Android, Mac, Windows, any device with a browser. No downloads, no installs, no app store gatekeeping.
